EarTranslator — Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026-06-12


SUMMARY
EarTranslator does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data.
All translation happens entirely on your device.


MICROPHONE / AUDIO
• The app accesses your microphone (including a connected Bluetooth earbud
  microphone) to translate speech as you speak.
• Audio is processed entirely on your device, in memory, only to perform live
  translation (speech recognition → translation → speech synthesis).
• Audio is never recorded to storage, never kept after translation, and never
  transmitted off the device.
• The app declares NO internet permission and is technically incapable of sending
  your audio or text anywhere.


BLUETOOTH
• Bluetooth permissions are used only to find paired audio devices (earbuds) and to
  route translated audio to the correct earbud.
• Bluetooth scanning is declared "neverForLocation"; the app does not use Bluetooth
  to derive or infer your location.


DATA WE COLLECT
None. No analytics, no advertising, no accounts, no network calls.


DATA WE SHARE
None.


CHILDREN
The app is a general-purpose utility and does not knowingly collect data from anyone,
including children.


ON-DEVICE FILES
The app stores only translation model files on the device. These contain no personal
data and are excluded from cloud backup.


CHANGES
If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new version
will be published at the same public URL used in the Play listing.


CONTACT
For questions about this policy, contact the support email listed on our Google Play
store listing.
