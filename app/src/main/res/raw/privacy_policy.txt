PRIVACY POLICY — EarTranslator
Effective date: June 12, 2026


SUMMARY
EarTranslator performs all translation on your device and does NOT collect, store,
transmit, or share any personal information. No accounts. No ads. No trackers. No
internet permission.


1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
None. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising identifiers, and no
data-transmitting crash reporting.


2. MICROPHONE & AUDIO
• Audio from your device or Bluetooth earbud microphone is processed only in temporary
  memory to recognize speech, translate it, and synthesize the translation.
• Audio is never written to storage, never kept after a phrase is processed, and never
  transmitted off your device.
• Speech recognition, translation, and text-to-speech all run locally on your device.


3. BLUETOOTH
Bluetooth permissions are used only to find paired earbuds and route translated audio to
the correct one. Scanning is flagged "neverForLocation"; the app does not use Bluetooth to
determine your location.


4. PERMISSIONS
• Microphone — capture speech to translate (core feature)
• Bluetooth — find and route audio to your earbuds
• Modify audio settings — manage the Bluetooth voice link
• Foreground service (microphone) — keep translating during a conversation


5. DATA SHARING
We do not share, sell, or disclose any data, because we collect none.


6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (COPPA)
The app is a general-purpose utility, is not directed to children under 13, and does not
knowingly collect data from anyone.


7. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR / CCPA)
Because no personal data is collected or processed, there is no personal data to access,
correct, delete, or port. We do not sell personal information.


8. SECURITY
All processing is local; no data leaves your device. The app declares no internet
permission and cannot transmit your audio or text.


9. CHANGES
Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above and posting the
revised policy at the URL in our Google Play listing.


10. CONTACT
EarTranslator Support — see the support email on our Google Play listing.
