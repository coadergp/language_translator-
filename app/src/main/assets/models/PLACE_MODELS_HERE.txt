Place ONNX model assets here following this exact layout (see ../../../../../README.md
for the commands that produce each file):

models/
  silero_vad.onnx
  whisper/
    encoder_model.onnx
    decoder_model.onnx
    vocab.json
  opus-mt/        (translation pivots through English: ship en-X and X-en only)
    en-es/
      encoder_model.onnx
      decoder_model.onnx
      source.spm
      target.spm
      vocab.json    (piece -> id map; required by the tokenizer)
    es-en/
      ...
  piper/
    en/
      en_US-lessac-medium.onnx
      en_US-lessac-medium.onnx.json
    es/
      es_ES-mls_10246-medium.onnx
      es_ES-mls_10246-medium.onnx.json
    ...

These files are intentionally NOT committed (they are large). The .onnx/.spm/.json
extensions are excluded from aapt compression in app/build.gradle.kts so they can be
mmap-loaded at runtime.
